Sloth Stew

  1. Mix sloth, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix eggs, milk, and oil.
  3. Stir both mixtures together.
  4. Bring to boil
  5. Simmer for 3 hours
  6. Enjoy
